<p><ul><li>Fit Tester</li><li>Integrity Tester</li><li>Performance Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mask tester market encompasses three primary types: Fit Testers, Integrity Testers, and Performance Testers. Fit Testers evaluate how well a mask seals to the wearer’s face, ensuring proper protection against contaminants. Integrity Testers assess the mask’s ability to maintain its barrier properties against specific substances. Performance Testers focus on measuring airflow resistance, filtration efficiency, and breathability, ensuring that masks meet safety standards and offer effective respiratory protection in various environments. Together, these testers enhance mask safety and reliability.</p></p>
