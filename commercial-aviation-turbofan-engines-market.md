<p><strong>The Commercial Aviation Turbofan Engines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-Bypass Turbofan Engines</li><li>Low-Bypass Turbofan Engines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial aviation turbofan engine market is primarily categorized into high-bypass and low-bypass turbofan engines. High-bypass turbofan engines, characterized by a large volume of air bypassing the engine core, are widely used in commercial aircraft for their fuel efficiency and reduced noise. In contrast, low-bypass turbofan engines, with more air passing through the engine core, are typically found in military and supersonic aircraft, offering higher thrust and speed but lower efficiency. Both types serve distinct operational needs in aviation.</p></p>
