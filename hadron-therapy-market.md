<p><p>Hadron therapy encompasses various particle beam types used for cancer treatment. Electron beam therapy employs electrons for surface tumors, while proton beam therapy targets deeper tissues with precise energy delivery, minimizing damage to surrounding healthy cells. Neutron beam therapy uses neutrons for specific cancers, particularly those resistant to conventional treatments. Carbon ion beam therapy offers high precision and effectiveness against difficult tumors. Alpha and beta particle beams explore innovative treatment avenues, with alpha beams targeting localized cancers and beta beams employed for specific radionuclide therapies.</p></p>
