<p><p>A fixed-gear bicycle, commonly referred to as a "fixie," is a type of bicycle that has a single gear and does not feature a freewheel mechanism. This design allows the rider to maintain a direct connection between the pedals and the rear wheel, resulting in a unique riding experience where cyclists can control their speed and braking by pedaling in reverse. The simplicity and lightweight nature of fixed-gear bicycles make them popular among urban riders and cycling enthusiasts.</p><p>The Fixed-Gear Bicycle Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing interest in sustainable transportation and fitness trends.
