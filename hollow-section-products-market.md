<p><p>The hollow section products market consists of three main types: hot-finished hollow sections, seamless hollow sections, and welded hollow sections. Hot-finished hollow sections are manufactured through high-temperature processes, enhancing strength and allowing for complex shapes. Seamless hollow sections are produced from solid billets, offering uniformity and resistance to pressure. Welded hollow sections are formed by welding flat steel plates, making them suitable for various applications due to their versatility and cost-effectiveness, particularly in construction and structural engineering.</p></p>
