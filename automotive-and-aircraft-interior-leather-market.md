<p><p>The automotive and aircraft interior leather market comprises three main types: pigmented leather, aniline leather, and semi-aniline leather. Pigmented leather is treated with a coating for durability and color uniformity, making it suitable for high-traffic areas. Aniline leather is dyed using soluble dyes, showcasing natural texture and breathability but is less resistant to stains. Semi-aniline leather combines features of both, offering a protective finish while retaining some natural appearance, balancing aesthetics and practicality for premium interior applications.</p></p>
