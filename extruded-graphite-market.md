<p><p>The extruded graphite market comprises various types, including pitch impregnated and un-impregnated forms. Pitch impregnated graphite features enhanced properties due to the infusion of pitch, improving strength and thermal conductivity, making it ideal for critical applications. Un-impregnated graphite is more porous, suitable for applications requiring lower density.
