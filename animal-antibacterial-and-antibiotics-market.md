<p><p>The animal antibacterial and antibiotics market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Tetracyclines** are broad-spectrum antibiotics effective against a range of gram-positive and gram-negative bacteria. </p><p>2. **Penicillins** target specific bacterial infections, commonly used in veterinary medicine. </p><p>3. **Sulfonamides** inhibit bacterial growth by interfering with folic acid synthesis. </p><p>4. **Macrolides** are effective against respiratory and soft tissue infections. </p><p>5. **Aminoglycosides** are potent against serious infections. </p><p>6. **Cephalosporins** have a broad spectrum and are used for various animal infections. Each type serves distinct therapeutic needs in veterinary care.</p></p>
