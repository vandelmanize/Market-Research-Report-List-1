<p><p>Dibasic esters (DBE) are versatile solvents and chemical intermediates derived from dibasic acids. The DBE market includes several types: Dimethyl Adipate (DMA), Dimethyl Glutarate (DMG), Dimethyl Succinate (DMS), and Mixed DBE. DMA is often used in coatings and adhesives; DMG serves in plasticizers and solvents; DMS finds applications in pharmaceuticals and agrochemicals. Mixed DBE encompasses a blend of these esters for broader utility in various industries, offering flexibility in formulation and application.</p></p>
