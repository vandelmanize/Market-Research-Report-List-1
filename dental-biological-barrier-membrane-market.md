<p><p>The Dental Biological Barrier Membrane market consists of two primary types: absorbable and non-absorbable membranes. Absorbable membranes are designed to dissolve over time, promoting natural tissue regeneration and eliminating the need for surgical removal, making them favorable for procedures like guided bone regeneration. Non-absorbable membranes, on the other hand, provide longer-term support and may require surgical removal after the healing process. Both types are essential for enhancing dental procedures, ensuring effective tissue management and promoting optimal patient outcomes.</p></p>
