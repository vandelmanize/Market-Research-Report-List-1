<p><p>The epoxy emulsifier market is categorized into two primary types: Oil in Water (O/W) and Water in Oil (W/O). O/W emulsifiers stabilize oil droplets dispersed in water, often used in coatings and paints, promoting easy application and quick drying. Conversely, W/O emulsifiers stabilize water droplets in oil, commonly utilized in cosmetics and heavy-duty lubricants, where moisture retention and longer shelf life are essential. Both types play critical roles in various industries by enhancing product performance and stability.</p></p>
