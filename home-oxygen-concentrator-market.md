<p><p>The Home Oxygen Concentrator market consists of two primary types: Portable Oxygen Concentrators (POCs) and Stationary Oxygen Concentrators (SOCs). POCs are lightweight, battery-operated devices designed for mobility, allowing users to maintain an active lifestyle while receiving oxygen therapy. SOCs, on the other hand, are larger, stationary units intended for home use, providing continuous oxygen supply for patients with higher needs. Both types enhance the quality of life for individuals with respiratory conditions, offering flexibility and personalized care solutions.</p></p>
