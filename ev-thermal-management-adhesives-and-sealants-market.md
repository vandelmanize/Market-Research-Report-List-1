<p><p>The EV Thermal Management Adhesives and Sealants market includes two primary types: gap fillers and structural adhesives. Gap fillers are designed to minimize thermal resistance and enhance heat dissipation between components, ensuring efficient thermal management in electric vehicles. Structural adhesives provide strong bonding between various parts, offering mechanical stability while also managing heat transfer. Together, these materials play a crucial role in optimizing performance, extending lifespan, and ensuring safety in electric vehicle systems by effectively managing thermal challenges.</p></p>
