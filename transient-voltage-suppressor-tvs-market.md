<p><p>Transient Voltage Suppressors (TVS) are essential devices used to protect electronic circuits from voltage spikes caused by events like lightning or electrostatic discharge. In the market, there are two primary types: Uni-polar and Bi-polar TVS. Uni-polar TVS are designed for unidirectional voltage clamping, ideal for DC applications, while Bi-polar TVS can handle bidirectional voltage spikes, making them suitable for AC applications or circuits with variable polarity. Both types are crucial for ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic components.</p></p>
