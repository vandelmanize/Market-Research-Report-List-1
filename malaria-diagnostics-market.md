<p><strong>The Malaria Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT)</li><li>Molecular Diagnostics</li><li>Microscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The malaria diagnostics market comprises three primary types: Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs), Molecular Diagnostics, and Microscopy. RDTs offer quick and easy detection of malaria antigens in blood samples, providing results in as little as 15 minutes. Molecular diagnostics, such as PCR, detect malaria DNA, ensuring high sensitivity and specificity. Microscopy involves examining blood smears under a microscope, allowing for the identification of malaria parasites. Together, these methods support effective malaria detection and treatment, catering to various healthcare settings.</p></p>
