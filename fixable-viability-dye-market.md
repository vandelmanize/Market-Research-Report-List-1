<p><p>Fixable Viability Dyes are specialized fluorescent dyes used in cell biology to distinguish live cells from dead ones in various applications, including flow cytometry, microscopy, and cell sorting. These dyes penetrate compromised cell membranes but are excluded from intact ones, allowing researchers to accurately assess cell viability and health. The growing emphasis on cell-based assays and the need for effective diagnostics contribute significantly to the expansion of the Fixable Viability Dye market.</p><p>The global Fixable Viability Dye Market is poised to experience substantial growth, estimated at a CAGR of 4.6% during the forecast period. Furthermore, a growing awareness of the importance of accurate cell viability assessment in clinical research and diagnostics supports market expansion.</p><p>Recent trends indicate a rising demand for multifunctional dyes that can provide more than just viability information, such as apoptosis detection and phenotypic characterization.
