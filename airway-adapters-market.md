<p><ul><li>Single Channel Adapters</li><li>Dual Channel Adapters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airway adapters market primarily includes two types: single channel adapters and dual channel adapters. Single channel adapters are designed for streamlined ventilation, allowing airflow through a single passage, typically used in simpler procedures or for patients with less severe airway complications. In contrast, dual channel adapters facilitate simultaneous airflow in two directions, improving ventilation efficiency and enabling advanced monitoring techniques in critical care settings. Both types are essential for enhancing patient safety and optimizing respiratory support during medical interventions.</p></p>
