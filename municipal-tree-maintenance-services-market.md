<p><strong>The Municipal Tree Maintenance Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Corrective Maintenance</li><li>Preventive Maintenance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Municipal tree maintenance services are categorized into corrective and preventive maintenance. Corrective maintenance involves addressing issues like disease, damage, or hazardous conditions after they occur, ensuring safety and tree health by removing dead limbs or entire trees when necessary. In contrast, preventive maintenance focuses on proactive measures, such as regular pruning, soil management, and pest control, to enhance tree vitality and reduce the likelihood of future problems. Both types are essential for sustainable urban forestry and public safety.</p></p>
