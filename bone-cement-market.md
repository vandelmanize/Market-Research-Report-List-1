<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity Cements</li><li>Medium Viscosity Cements</li><li>High Viscosity Cements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone cement market is categorized into low, medium, and high viscosity cements, each tailored for specific surgical applications. Low viscosity cements flow easily, making them ideal for precise injections in minimally invasive procedures. Medium viscosity cements offer a balanced combination of flow and stability, suitable for a variety of orthopedic surgeries. High viscosity cements, with their thicker consistency, provide superior strength and support, often used in load-bearing applications. Together, these types cater to diverse clinical needs within orthopedics.</p></p>
