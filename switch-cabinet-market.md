Low Voltage Switch Cabinets are utilized in residential and commercial settings, managing circuits up to 1,000 volts. Medium Voltage Switch Cabinets operate between 1 kV and 36 kV, often found in industrial and utility applications for less frequent switching. High Voltage Switch Cabinets, exceeding 36 kV, are essential for power transmission and distribution in electric utilities, ensuring safe management of electrical grids and infrastructure.</p></p>
