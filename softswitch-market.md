<p><ul><li>Class 4 Softswitches</li><li>Class 5 Softswitches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Softswitch market is divided into Class 4 and Class 5 types based on functionality. Class 4 Softswitches primarily handle trunking and manage large volumes of voice traffic between carrier networks, focusing on routing and call management. In contrast, Class 5 Softswitches cater to end-users, offering features like call waiting, voicemail, and billing, serving retail providers. Both types facilitate efficient communication, but their applications differ, with Class 4 supporting wholesale services and Class 5 targeting consumer-centric functionalities.</p></p>
