<p><p>The Hall Current and Voltage Sensor market is categorized into two main types: Analog Output Hall Sensors and Digital Output Hall Sensors. Analog Output Hall Sensors provide a continuous voltage signal proportional to the magnetic field strength, enabling precise measurement of current or voltage. In contrast, Digital Output Hall Sensors deliver discrete signals, typically indicating the presence or absence of a magnetic field, making them suitable for on/off applications. Both types are essential for various industrial and automotive applications, ensuring effective monitoring and control.</p></p>
