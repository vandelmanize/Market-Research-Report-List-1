<p><p>Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) technology encompasses various market types, including Targeted Sequencing, Whole Exome Sequencing, and Whole Genome Sequencing. Targeted Sequencing focuses on specific genes or regions of interest, enabling precise analysis and reduced costs. Whole Exome Sequencing examines the exonic regions of the genome, which are crucial for protein coding and primarily associated with diseases. Whole Genome Sequencing provides a comprehensive analysis of an entire genome, revealing complete genomic information for research, clinical applications, and personalized medicine.</p></p>
