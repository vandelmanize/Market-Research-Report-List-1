<p><p>The Integrated Passive Devices (IPD) market encompasses several types, each serving specific functions. ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) devices protect sensitive electronics from voltage spikes. EMI (Electromagnetic Interference) filters ensure signal integrity by mitigating unwanted interference. RF-IPD (Radio Frequency Integrated Passive Devices) are tailored for wireless communications, enhancing performance in RF applications. Other markets may include applications like power management and energy harvesting. Together, these components streamline designs, reduce footprint, and improve overall device reliability in electronic systems.</p></p>
