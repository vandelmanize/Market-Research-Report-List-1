<p><p>The Aluminium Cleaner market features two primary types: phosphoric acid-based and glycolic acid-based cleaners. Phosphoric acid cleaners effectively remove rust, scale, and oxidation from aluminium surfaces, promoting adhesion for coatings. They are often used in industrial applications. Glycolic acid-based cleaners, on the other hand, offer a gentler yet efficient option, suitable for more delicate surfaces, providing a thorough clean while minimizing damage. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, ensuring proper maintenance of aluminium components.</p></p>
