<p><strong>The Peripheral Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Balloon-expandable</li><li>Self-expandable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The peripheral stents market is divided into two primary types: balloon-expandable and self-expandable stents. Balloon-expandable stents are deployed using a balloon catheter that expands at the target site, providing precise placement and immediate vessel support upon inflation. Conversely, self-expandable stents utilize a material that allows them to expand autonomously when deployed, adapting to the vessel's shape and offering durability. Both types are crucial in treating peripheral artery diseases, enhancing blood flow and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
