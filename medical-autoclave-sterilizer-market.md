<p><p>The medical autoclave sterilizer market is primarily divided into two types: horizontal and vertical autoclave sterilizers. Horizontal autoclaves have a side-opening design, typically used for larger loads and are common in hospitals and laboratories. Vertical autoclaves, with a top-opening design, are often used for smaller volumes or in settings where space is limited. Both types utilize steam under pressure to eliminate pathogens, ensuring equipment and instruments are properly sterilized for safe medical use.</p></p>
