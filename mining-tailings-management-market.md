<p><p>Mining tailings management involves various methods to handle waste materials produced during ore processing. Dry stacking refers to dewatering tailings to create a stackable material, reducing water usage and minimizing environmental impact. Underground storage entails transporting tailings to abandoned mines or deep underground facilities, limiting surface footprint and potential contamination. Other methods may include impoundment or mixed approaches that combine several techniques to enhance sustainability, safety, and efficiency in managing mining byproducts while protecting ecosystems.</p></p>
