<p><p>The automotive glass market for windshields includes several types, primarily tempered and laminated glass. Tempered glass is heat-treated for strength and shatters into small, blunt pieces, enhancing safety. Laminated glass consists of two layers of glass with a plastic interlayer, providing additional security and sound insulation, and remains intact when shattered. Other types may include UV-filtering and tinted glass, contributing to vehicle aesthetics and passenger comfort. These variations cater to diverse automotive safety and performance requirements.</p></p>
