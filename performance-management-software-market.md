<p><p>Performance management software is categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on a company’s servers, offering greater control over data and customization, but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on external servers, providing easier access, scalability, and automatic updates, making it ideal for remote teams. Organizations choose between these options based on factors like budget, security, and specific performance management needs.</p></p>
