<p><p>The glutathione tablet market comprises two main types: reduced glutathione and oxidized glutathione. Reduced glutathione is a potent antioxidant in its active form, commonly used for detoxification, immune support, and skin health. In contrast, oxidized glutathione, formed when reduced glutathione donates electrons, lacks the same antioxidant properties but can play a role in detoxification processes and cellular signaling. Together, these forms address various health needs, catering to consumers seeking both preventative and restorative benefits.</p></p>
