<p><p>The sewage sampler market is primarily divided into two types: manual samplers and automatic samplers. Manual samplers require operators to collect samples manually at predetermined intervals, often resulting in variability in sample quality due to human error. In contrast, automatic samplers utilize programmable systems to collect samples consistently and at specific times, enhancing reliability and accuracy. Both types serve critical roles in wastewater management, enabling effective monitoring and analysis of sewage for environmental compliance and pollution control.</p></p>
