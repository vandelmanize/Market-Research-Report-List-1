In contrast, ultrasonic machines utilize high-frequency sound waves to create microscopic bubbles that remove dirt and grime from grooves more thoroughly and gently. Both methods cater to audiophiles seeking to enhance sound quality and prolong the life of their vinyl collections.</p></p>
