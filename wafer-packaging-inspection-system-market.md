<p><ul><li>Optical Based</li><li>Infrared Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wafer Packaging Inspection System market comprises two primary types: Optical Based and Infrared Type. Optical Based systems utilize cameras and imaging technology to detect surface defects and ensure correct alignment during packaging. They offer high-resolution imaging for precise inspection. In contrast, Infrared Type systems leverage thermal imaging to identify issues related to heat distribution and material properties, enabling the detection of defects that are not visible through standard optical means. Both types are essential for quality assurance in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
