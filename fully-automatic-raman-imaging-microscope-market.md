<p><strong>The Fully Automatic Raman Imaging Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmissive</li><li>Diffraction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fully Automatic Raman Imaging Microscope market includes two primary types: Transmissive and Diffraction. Transmissive Raman microscopy utilizes light that passes through a sample, allowing for high-resolution imaging of transparent or semi-transparent materials. In contrast, Diffraction-based Raman imaging leverages the interference of light to capture detailed spectral information, enhancing the spatial resolution for complex samples. Both types are pivotal in various applications, including materials science, pharmaceuticals, and biological research, facilitating advanced imaging and analysis of molecular compositions.</p></p>
