<p><p>The Non-Biodegradable Recycled Thermoplastics market comprises various forms, including flakes, pellets, and granulates. Flakes are often produced from shredded plastic waste and serve as a raw material for further processing. Pellets are uniform-sized pieces created by melting and re-extruding flakes, enabling efficient handling and manufacturing. Granulates are larger particles used in injection molding and extrusion processes. These forms facilitate the recycling of thermoplastics, contributing to sustainability by reducing waste and promoting resource efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
