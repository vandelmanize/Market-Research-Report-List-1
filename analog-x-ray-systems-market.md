<p><strong>The Analog X-ray Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Film Based</li><li>CR Film Cassette</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog X-ray systems encompass various market types, primarily categorized into film-based systems and CR (Computed Radiography) film cassette systems. Film-based systems utilize traditional X-ray films to capture images, requiring chemical processing for development. In contrast, CR film cassette systems employ digital technology to convert captured X-ray images into a digital format, enabling easier storage and sharing. Both types serve essential roles in medical imaging, but the CR systems offer enhanced efficiency and image manipulation capabilities compared to conventional film.</p></p>
