<p><p>The mobile speech recognition software market comprises three main types: Isolated Word Recognition, Keyword Spotting, and Continuous Speech Recognition. Isolated Word Recognition focuses on recognizing discrete words or phrases, ideal for applications requiring clear commands. Keyword Spotting detects specific keywords within continuous speech, enabling efficient interaction in various contexts. Continuous Speech Recognition processes natural, flowing speech, allowing seamless conversation and dictation capabilities. Each type serves distinct use cases, from simple voice commands to advanced voice assistants in mobile devices.</p></p>
