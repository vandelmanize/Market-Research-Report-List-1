<p><ul><li>Frog Type Dynamic Compactor</li><li>Vibration Type Dynamic Compactor</li><li>Tamping Type Dynamic Compactor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dynamic compactor market includes three main types: frog type, vibration type, and tamping type. Frog type dynamic compactors use a heavy weight dropped from a significant height to achieve compaction, ideal for dense materials. Vibration type compactors employ high-frequency vibrations to enhance soil density and stability, suitable for various soil types. Tamping type dynamic compactors utilize a combination of vertical and lateral movement to achieve effective compaction, often used in trench and road work applications. Each type caters to specific soil conditions and engineering requirements.</p></p>
