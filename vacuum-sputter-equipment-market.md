<p><p>The vacuum sputter equipment market comprises different types, including DC sputtering, RF sputtering, and others. DC sputtering uses direct current to deposit films, ideal for conductive materials. RF sputtering employs radio frequency power, suitable for non-conductive substrates, enhancing versatility. Other market types may include magnetron sputtering and bias sputtering, each catering to specific applications and materials. Together, these technologies serve various industries, such as electronics, optics, and automotive, facilitating advanced thin-film deposition processes.</p></p>
