<p><p>The impellers market is categorized into three types based on their shear capabilities. Low-shear impellers are designed for gentle mixing, ideal for delicate materials and minimizing damage. Medium-shear impellers offer a balance, commonly used for mixtures requiring moderate dispersion and emulsification. High-shear impellers provide intense mixing, suitable for homogenizing thick or viscous fluids, greatly enhancing particle size reduction and emulsification. Each type serves distinct industrial applications, catering to varying processing needs across sectors like pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals.</p></p>
